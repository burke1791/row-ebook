How does this work?

After you submit the above form, you will receive a message asking you to verify your email address. Once verified, you will receive a weekly email with your desired ebook format(s) attached. Each new ebook file will contain all of the released chapters, so you will want to delete older versions off your device before loading the new one.

Can I use my "Send to Kindle" email address?

No. The email service I use only allows me to send to "verified" email addresses, and I have not figured out a way to verify these types of emails.

Will you delete my email address after the book is released, or if I unsubscribe?

Yes.

The latest chapter was released several hours ago, where is my updated ebook?

Please be patient. I have to find the URL for the new chapters and manually add it to the script that generates the ebook files. If I'm swamped at work, I may not have time to do this until the evening.
